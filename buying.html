Welcome to the Buying Guide

This webpage will help you understand what brands are the most reputable

When buying a RC-plane, you want to know what parts it comes with and what it requires. Normally it would be a plane that does not come with the remote. transmitter and battery therefore you need to buy it separately. Some are only kits, therefore you need to buy a whole plane with it as well. Some people prefer to build their own planes out of either balsa wood or foam with a electronic kit for RC planes.

Remote control airplanes are a popular hobby and chances are you have a well stocked local store. However there are several brands that have a good reputation and are available internationally. Probably the largest brand is Horizon Hobby, they manufacture everything from RTF(Ready to Fly) to

Horizon Hobby Website

While Horizon Hobby does produce transmitters and recievers, the more reputable brand for these is Spektrum. Spektrum manufacture what could be considered the best radio equipment available for remote control aircarft. Most BNF's(bind and fly) come with spektrum recievers installed due to the best range and reliability. Spektrums transmitters are also highly reliable and robust.
